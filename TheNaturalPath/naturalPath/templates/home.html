About this site

This site is for anyone who has seen a traditional MD and have had no luck.

Naturopaths can help with many different conditions, like MDs, so are well equipped to handle most medical concerns that you may have.

This site is a resource where naturopaths and naturopathic doctors have registered there practices, to help people improve their health.

What is a naturopathic doctor?

A naturopathic doctor (ND) is a healthcare professional who emphasizes natural and holistic approaches to healing, combining traditional and alternative therapies to promote overall well-being and address the root causes of health issues.

naturopathic doctors are also well versed in Western medicinal treatments, and can provide such services if necessary.

An ND could be a good fit for you if you've found that conventional medicine hasn't worked as you would like.
